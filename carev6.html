Audi Q6 e-tron

Rs.1 Cr


Expected Launch Date : Mar 15, 2025

It is expected to be launched in India by March 2025 In international markets, it uses a 94.9 kWh battery pack for all of its three variants. The top two trims are equipped with a dual-motor setup and all-wheel-drive producing 517 PS on the SQ6 e-tron, and 387 PS on the Q6 e-tron Quattro, while the base variant gets a single-motor setup with rear-wheel-drive producing 326 PS. Depending on the variant chosen, it offers a claimed driving range between 598 km to 641 km.
