Hyundai Creta Electric

Rs.17.99 - 24.38 Lakh


The Hyundai Creta EV is an all-electric compact SUV that can seat up to five people. It follows the same design language as its internal combustion engine (ICE) counterpart, but with some EV-specific design elements, including a redesigned grille and aerodynamically styled alloy wheels. The Creta EV offers a claimed range of up to 473 km.
